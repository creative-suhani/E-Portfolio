Lab 2 — Assignment Briefing & E-Portfolio Instructions

Date: 30 July, 2025   •   Course Code: AET 501 / SET 501

Details about assignments, e-portfolio requirements and final submission timelines.

Summary

In the second lab session the teacher discussed the semester assignments and the e-portfolio submission requirements. The session explained why an online portfolio is important and how to organise your work for final submission in October.

Assignment details

  • Assignment 1: Create a professional LinkedIn profile (complete with a headline, summary, and experience/education entries).
  • Assignment 2: Record and submit a Self-Introduction video showcasing the preparation checklist discussed in class.

E-Portfolio requirements & timeline

  • Design and host an E-Portfolio website where all lab learnings, assignments and artifacts will be uploaded.
  • Include links to your LinkedIn profile and the submitted self-introduction video inside the portfolio.
  • Maintain clear folder structure or pages for each lab and assignment to make evaluation straightforward.
  • Final E-Portfolio submission deadline: October (confirm exact date with the teacher).

Tips shared in class

  1. Keep your LinkedIn headline short, specific and role-focused.
  2. Record the self-intro video in a quiet place with clear lighting and a neutral background.
  3. Use concise bullet points and visuals in your e-portfolio to highlight key projects and learnings.
