Lab 3 — Speech & Introduction to Unit 3

Date: 6 August, 2025   •   Course Code: AET 501 / SET 501

Details about the speaking activity and an introduction to the core concepts of Organizational Behavior.

Summary

In the third lab session, our teacher explained speech delivery techniques — covering structure, tone, clarity, and audience engagement. We learned how to prepare and present speeches effectively, focusing on confidence and clear communication.

Introduction to Unit 3: Concept of OB

  • Motivation: Importance, types, and effect on work behavior.
  • Groups in Organizations: Group decision-making techniques, teamwork, and interpersonal relations.
  • Leadership: Meaning, importance, and the difference between leaders and managers.

Interactive Speaking Activity

At the end of the class, an interactive speaking activity was conducted. A boy from the class gave small topics to each student, and everyone had to speak about their assigned topic on the spot. This helped us improve our impromptu speaking skills and boosted confidence in expressing ideas spontaneously.
