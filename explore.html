About Me

I am a passionate graphic designer with a keen eye for aesthetics and a drive to create impactful visual stories. My journey began with a love for sketching and has evolved into a full-time pursuit of digital design. I specialize in branding, UI/UX, and visual storytelling. I believe that good design is not just about making things look beautiful, but about making them meaningful and functional.

Creations & Achievements

My Projects

Surreal Apple Manipulation

A digital art piece exploring surrealism through photographic manipulation. This project blends a realistic apple with dreamlike elements to create a captivating and thought-provoking visual narrative.

Noir Film Poster Design

The official movie poster design for an upcoming noir thriller. This work focuses on creating a sense of mystery and tension using dramatic lighting, stylized typography, and a minimalist color palette.

Coffee Bean Photography

A photographic study of coffee. This series captures the intricate details and rich textures of coffee beans, showcasing the beauty in everyday objects through macro photography and selective focus.

Strawberry Still Life

A vibrant still life photograph featuring a strawberry. The project highlights the contrast between the fresh fruit and its textured environment, using lighting to emphasize color and form.
