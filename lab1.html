Lab 1 — Career Skills

Date: 26th July, 2025   •   Course Code: AET 501 / SET 501

Overview of the course, syllabus, and first in-class practice of self-introductions.

Summary

In the first lab session the teacher introduced the subject Career Skills / Soft Skills & Personality Development — III and walked us through the full syllabus and expected learning outcomes. The session focused on improving self-introduction, communication, teamwork, public speaking, decision-making and interview preparation.

Topics / Modules covered (overview)

  • Self Introduction – II — preparation checklist and video presentation
  • Art of Conversation – II — vote of thanks, speeches, and small talk practice
  • Concept of OB — motivation, team-building, leadership basics
  • Public Speaking – II — extempore, JAM sessions, story-building and pitch practice
  • Interview Skills — mock interviews, checklists, HR & technical round preparation

In-class activity

Toward the end of the class, each student gave a short self-introduction to begin practising Unit 1 skills in a live setting. This activity helped students receive quick feedback and identify areas to improve before recording the formal self-introduction video.

Learning outcomes

  1. Build a concise and confident self-introduction (both spoken and recorded).
  2. Understand basic public speaking and conversational techniques.
  3. Recognise components of teamwork and leadership in applied scenarios.
  4. Prepare for mock interviews and identify common evaluation criteria.
